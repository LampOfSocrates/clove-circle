What We Offer

Our Services

Evidence-based sustainability consulting to help your organisation navigate the path to net zero and beyond.

Core Services

How We Help

Clove Circle provides expert guidance across the full sustainability spectrum, from strategy to assessment to reporting.

Net Zero Strategies

Clove Circle provides expert guidance to help organizations achieve carbon neutrality through innovative and effective net zero strategies.

  • Science-based target setting
  • Decarbonisation roadmaps
  • Scope 1, 2 & 3 emissions accounting
  • Implementation planning

Life Cycle Assessment

We offer comprehensive life cycle assessments to help businesses understand and improve the sustainability of their products and services.

  • ISO 14040/14044 compliant LCAs
  • Cradle-to-grave analysis
  • Environmental product declarations
  • Hotspot identification

Sustainability Solutions

Our sustainability solutions are designed to drive positive environmental impact and ensure long-term success for our clients.

  • ESG strategy & governance
  • Sustainability reporting (GRI, TCFD)
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Performance benchmarking
Additional Capabilities

Further Expertise

Beyond our core offerings, Clove Circle provides a range of specialist services to address your unique sustainability challenges.

Circular Economy

Strategies to minimise waste, extend product life cycles, and close resource loops — moving towards a regenerative business model.

Carbon Reporting

Robust greenhouse gas inventories and disclosure reports aligned with GHG Protocol, SECR, and other international frameworks.

Sustainable Design

Embedding sustainability principles into product and process design from the earliest stages to maximise environmental benefit.

Techno-Economic Analysis

Combined technical and economic evaluation of sustainability investments to support evidence-based decision making.

Training & Capacity Building

Workshops and training programmes to equip your teams with the knowledge and skills to embed sustainability across your organisation.

Research Partnerships

Collaborative research projects bridging academic expertise and industry application in sustainability and environmental science.
