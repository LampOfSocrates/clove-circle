Who We Are

About Clove Circle

Clove Circle is a leading consulting firm specialising in net zero strategies, life cycle assessment, and sustainability solutions. Founded by Professor Jhuma Sadhukhan, we are dedicated to helping businesses achieve their sustainability goals through innovative and evidence-based strategies.

Our mission is to provide top-notch consulting services that drive positive environmental impact and business success. We work closely with our clients to develop and implement customised solutions that address their specific needs and challenges.
