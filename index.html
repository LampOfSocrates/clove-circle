Who We Are

About Clove Circle

Founded by Professor Jhuma Sadhukhan, Clove Circle is a specialist consultancy dedicated to helping organisations achieve genuine, measurable sustainability outcomes.

We bring together scientific rigour and practical consulting expertise. Our team offers deep experience across sectors — from manufacturing and energy to food systems and public policy — delivering clear analysis, credible reporting, and actionable strategies aligned with your business objectives and stakeholder expectations.

Our mission is to provide top-notch consulting services that drive positive environmental impact and business success. We work closely with our clients to develop and implement customised solutions that address their specific needs and challenges.
