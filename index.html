About Clove Circle

Clove Circle is a leading consulting firm specialising in net zero strategies, life cycle assessment, and sustainability solutions. Founded by Professor Jhuma Sadhukhan, we are dedicated to helping businesses achieve their sustainability goals through innovative, evidence-based strategies.

Our mission is to provide top-notch consulting services that drive positive environmental impact and business success. We work closely with our clients to develop and implement customised solutions that address their specific needs and challenges.

Why Choose Us?

At Clove Circle, we bring together scientific rigour and practical consulting expertise. Our team offers deep experience across sectors — from manufacturing and energy to food systems and public policy — delivering clear analysis, credible reporting, and actionable strategies aligned with your business objectives and stakeholder expectations.
